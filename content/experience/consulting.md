## How I Work

I'm not a vendor selling a product. I'm a builder who understands your domain well enough to create something tailored. Every engagement follows the same pattern: understand the actual problem, identify where AI creates genuine leverage, build the minimum viable solution, and iterate based on real usage.

## What I Build

**Custom AI pipelines for specialized workflows.** Government procurement tracking. Document intelligence for paper-heavy offices. Financial coaching systems. Not generic chatbots — purpose-built tools that solve domain-specific problems.

**Self-hosted AI infrastructure.** Organizations that need AI capabilities but can't (or shouldn't) send their data to third-party APIs. I design and deploy local inference systems that provide enterprise-grade AI while keeping your data behind your firewall.

**Practical automation.** The unglamorous but high-value work of automating repetitive processes. Email summarization. Document classification. Workflow triggers. The stuff that saves 30 minutes a day for every person on your team.

### Ongoing Partnership
- Monthly system optimization and feature development
- Knowledge base updates and model fine-tuning

## Ideal Clients

**Government agencies** with document-heavy workflows and strict data sovereignty requirements. I understand the constraints: limited budgets, compliance requirements, change-resistant culture, and the need for solutions that non-technical staff can actually use.

**Small to mid-size businesses** that need AI capabilities but can't justify enterprise pricing. Professional services firms, specialized contractors, regional healthcare providers — organizations with clear use cases but practical budget limitations.

**Organizations with proprietary data** that can't use cloud-based AI services. Financial institutions, legal firms, healthcare organizations, and any business where keeping data on-premises isn't just preferred — it's required.
